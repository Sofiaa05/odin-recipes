Lasagna!

World's best Lasagna!

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly!

This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Lasagna noodles
  • An onion and two cloves of garlic
  • A can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste.
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Egg

Directions to prepare Lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
